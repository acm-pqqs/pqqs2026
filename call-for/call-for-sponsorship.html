Support ACM PQQS 2026 and showcase your leadership in quantum-safe technologies.

About ACM PQQS

The ACM Conference on Post-Quantum and Quantum-based Security (PQQS) is an annual venue dedicated to the most urgent advances in quantum-resilient security and practical post-quantum migration. The conference covers both quantum-enabled security systems and post-quantum cryptographic migration, integrating theory, systems, deployment, and standardization perspectives.

PQQS follows the ACM Policy Against Harassment at ACM Activities. Please familiarize yourself with the ACM Policy Against Harassment.
